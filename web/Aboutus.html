About Us

Welcome to LAVISH DECOR, one source for all the furniture and decor items. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of furnitures and decor, with an emphasis on quality, comfort and affordability. Founded in 2003 by Steve Menson, LAVISH DECOR has come a long way from its beginnings in Paris. When Steve Menson first started out, his passion for providing best decor service drove them to start their own business. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, LAVISH DECOR
